## 1. Target Industries

AVIVO's use cases and product features are specifically designed for the following industries:

### 1.1 Pharmaceutical & Life Sciences
**Why they're a prime target:**
- Pharma companies manage an extraordinarily high volume of contracts — clinical trial agreements, CRO contracts, CMO agreements, regulatory submissions, licensing deals, distribution contracts, supplier agreements
- Regulatory compliance is non-negotiable — FDA, EMA, HIPAA, GDPR, GxP all impose strict contract documentation and obligation management requirements
- Missed contractual deadlines in clinical trials can delay drug launches by months, costing hundreds of millions of dollars
- IP licensing in pharma is extremely high-value and clause-sensitive — errors carry catastrophic risk

**AVIVO fit:** Risk management, alert management, compliance reporting, and document intelligence are tailor-made for pharma's regulatory burden.

**Use case cited by AVIVO:** "A large pharmaceutical company managing hundreds of contracts across different regions needs to ensure compliance with local regulations while minimising risk exposure."

### 1.2 Financial Services & Banking
**Why they're a prime target:**
- Banks, insurance companies, asset managers, and fintech firms handle enormous volumes of financial contracts — loan agreements, investment mandates, counterparty agreements, vendor contracts, employment contracts
- Regulatory compliance requirements (RBI, SEBI in India; SEC/FINRA in the US; FCA in the UK) demand meticulous contract documentation
- Executive decision-making depends heavily on contract data (pricing terms, renewal windows, performance conditions) — currently retrieved manually at great cost

**AVIVO fit:** AVIVO Intel's conversational Q&A interface directly addresses the executive access problem. Risk management scores contracts for financial exposure.

**Use case cited by AVIVO:** "An executive team at a financial services firm requires quick access to contract data to inform critical business decisions, but manual searches through contract databases are time-consuming."

### 1.3 Professional Services (Consulting, Auditing, Legal, IT Services)
**Why they're a prime target:**
- Professional services firms deal with large numbers of client service agreements, engagement letters, master service agreements, and sub-contractor agreements
- Multiple departments (client-facing, legal, finance) all need access to contract terms — creating coordination bottlenecks
- Long manual review cycles slow down deal closure, costing revenue

**AVIVO fit:** Contract creation + comparison + approval automation cuts cycle times. AVIVO Intel enables self-service contract data access across departments.

**Use case cited by AVIVO:** "A professional services firm dealing with complex service agreements across multiple departments struggles with long contract cycles due to manual reviews and approvals."

### 1.4 Technology Companies (SaaS, Product Companies, IT Firms)
**Why they're a prime target:**
- Fast-growing tech companies enter new partnerships, vendor agreements, and customer contracts at high velocity
- Legal teams are typically lean — they cannot afford slow, manual contract cycles
- Standard SaaS subscription agreements, partner contracts, and vendor MSAs are highly repeatable — ideal for template-based automation

**AVIVO fit:** AI-assisted contract creation with pre-approved clause libraries dramatically reduces drafting time. Real-time collaboration enables legal + sales + finance to work simultaneously.

**Use case cited by AVIVO:** "A fast-growing tech company that frequently negotiates contracts with partners and vendors faces delays due to lengthy back-and-forth negotiations and manual drafting."

### 1.5 Global Enterprises with Complex Supply Chains (Procurement/Vendor Management)
**Why they're a prime target:**
- Multinational companies managing hundreds of vendor contracts across geographies need a single source of truth
- Inconsistency in vendor contract terms creates compliance exposure and negotiation disadvantages
- Procurement teams need visibility into contract terms, pricing, SLAs, and renewal dates across a fragmented supplier base

**AVIVO fit:** Central contract repository + AI-driven insights + automated alerts covers the entire vendor management contract lifecycle.

**Use case cited by AVIVO:** "A global company managing multiple vendor contracts needs to streamline its vendor management process and ensure that all contracts are consistent, up-to-date, and compliant."

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Firmographic Profile

| Dimension | Target |
|---|---|
| **Company Size** | Mid-market to large enterprise (100+ employees, ideally 500+) |
| **Contract Volume** | Companies managing 100+ active contracts simultaneously |
| **Industry** | Pharma, Financial Services, Professional Services, Technology, Global Enterprises |
| **Geography** | US (primary, given founder's base), India (development hub), global enterprise clients |
| **Digital Maturity** | Organisations already invested in digital transformation — using cloud tools, open to AI adoption |
| **Pain Level** | High — companies where manual contract management is already causing measurable delays, errors, or costs |

### Technographic Profile
AVIVO likely targets companies that are:
- Currently using **spreadsheets or basic document management tools** (SharePoint, Google Drive) for contract storage — indicating an unsophisticated baseline ripe for upgrade
- **NOT** already on a full enterprise CLM system (Icertis, Ironclad) — those are already locked in
- Using **cloud infrastructure** (AWS, Azure, GCP) indicating willingness to adopt cloud SaaS

### Persona Profiles (Who Buys AVIVO)

#### Persona 1: General Counsel / Chief Legal Officer
- **Pain:** Team buried in contract review; needs to show efficiency gains to the board
- **AVIVO Value:** Reduces manual review time; provides risk scoring; enables compliance reporting
- **Buying trigger:** A compliance failure or near-miss that highlights the risk of manual processes

#### Persona 2: Chief Procurement Officer / VP Procurement
- **Pain:** No single view of vendor obligations; missed renewal deadlines cost money
- **AVIVO Value:** Central repository + alerts + vendor contract insights
- **Buying trigger:** A missed contract renewal that resulted in unwanted auto-renewal or price increase

#### Persona 3: VP Legal Operations / Legal Ops Manager
- **Pain:** Legal team overwhelmed with routine contract drafting and comparison work
- **AVIVO Value:** Automation of creation and comparison frees up lawyers for high-value work
- **Buying trigger:** Growing contract volume without proportional growth in legal headcount

#### Persona 4: Chief Financial Officer
- **Pain:** Financial commitments buried in contracts; no visibility into upcoming payment obligations or pricing changes
- **AVIVO Value:** AVIVO Intel enables instant retrieval of financial terms; alerts flag payment milestones
- **Buying trigger:** A missed contractual payment or financial surprise traced back to poor contract visibility

#### Persona 5: Chief Information Officer / VP of Digital Transformation
- **Pain:** The organisation needs an enterprise AI use case that shows immediate productivity ROI
- **AVIVO Value:** Document intelligence and contract AI is one of the highest-ROI AI applications in the enterprise
- **Buying trigger:** Board mandate to implement AI and demonstrate productivity improvements

## 3. Customer Segments by Product

### AVIVO Contract Buyers
Primarily legal, procurement, and operations teams in enterprises with:
- High contract creation velocity (multiple new contracts per week)
- Complex multi-party contracts requiring extensive negotiation
- Regulatory compliance obligations embedded in contracts
- Significant cost of legal errors (pharma, finance, regulated industries)

### AVIVO Intel Buyers
Broader than AVIVO Contract — any department that:
- Manages a large volume of documents and struggles with information retrieval
- Has knowledge workers who spend significant time searching for information
- Needs executives to make faster, data-driven decisions based on document intelligence
- Wants to build an internal AI-powered knowledge base

AVIVO Intel has a **wider addressable market** than AVIVO Contract because document intelligence is relevant to virtually every department (HR, Finance, Operations, Legal, R&D, etc.), whereas CLM is primarily a Legal/Procurement tool.
