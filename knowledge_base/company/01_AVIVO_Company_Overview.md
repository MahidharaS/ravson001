## 3. What AVIVO Actually Does

AVIVO is a **B2B AI SaaS company** operating at the intersection of **Legal Technology (LegalTech)**, **Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM)**, and **Enterprise Document Intelligence**. They build two core AI-powered software products:

### Product 1 — AVIVO Contract
*"Enhance productivity and improve cost savings by automatically creating contracts."*

AVIVO Contract is a full-stack **Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM)** platform that uses generative AI and NLP to automate, streamline, and intelligently manage the entire contracting process — from drafting a contract from scratch through negotiation, approval, compliance, and archival.

It addresses three core pain points:
- Traditional contract creation is error-prone and slow
- Long contract review/approval cycles block business operations
- Ensuring regulatory compliance and mitigating risk manually is unsustainable at scale

### Product 2 — AVIVO Intel
*"Accelerate decision making by getting instant answers to your questions."*

AVIVO Intel is a **Document Intelligence & Knowledge Management** platform. It allows organisations to store all their documents (not just contracts) in a secure repository and then **query them using a conversational AI chatbot** to extract actionable insights instantly — without reading entire documents.

It addresses three core pain points:
- Employees waste enormous time searching for information buried in documents
- Fragmented information causes project delays and missed decisions
- Teams struggle to convert raw document data into actionable intelligence

## 4. Core Product Features — In Depth

### AVIVO Contract Features

#### 4.1 Contract Creation
- Extensive pre-built template library
- AI-assisted content generation (clauses, terms, obligations auto-suggested)
- Real-time collaboration tools (multi-user editing)
- Robust version control with full audit trail

#### 4.2 Contract Comparison
- Detailed text and clause-level comparison between two contract versions
- Visual representation of changes (additions, deletions, modifications highlighted)
- Useful for redlining, renegotiation rounds, and policy adherence checks

#### 4.3 Management of Risk
- Automatic risk identification and scoring — the AI reads a contract and flags risky clauses
- Compliance checks against regulatory requirements and internal policies
- Compliance reporting for audit purposes

#### 4.4 Alert Management
- Real-time notifications for critical contractual events (renewal dates, payment milestones, obligation deadlines)
- Customisable alert criteria — users can define what triggers a notification
- Centralised alert dashboard giving a bird's-eye view of all pending actions

### AVIVO Intel Features

#### 4.5 Document Repository
- Secure, centralised storage for multiple document formats (PDF, Word, Excel, etc.)
- Advanced organisation with tagging, metadata labelling, and folder structures
- Role-based access control — employees only see documents relevant to their role

#### 4.6 Document Intelligence
- Automated extraction of key data points from unstructured documents
- AI-generated summaries and contextual insights
- Conversational chatbot interface — users type questions in plain English and get instant answers
